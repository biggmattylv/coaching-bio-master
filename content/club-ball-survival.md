# The Club Ball Survival Guide
## 10 Questions to Ask Before You Sign

Joining a club team is a major commitment. Don't go in blind. Use these 10 questions to ensure you're getting value for your money and the right environment for your athlete.

### 1. The Philosophy
"Is this organization focused on player development, or are we just chasing rings?"
*   *Why it matters:* You want a team that builds skills, not just one that plays for trophies.

### 2. The Commitment
"What’s the real travel schedule, and how do we handle weekends off?"
*   *Why it matters:* Burnout is real. Know the expectations before you commit.

### 3. The Costs
"Give me the total breakdown—fees, uniforms, travel, and the 'hidden' extras."
*   *Why it matters:* Avoid surprises mid-season.

### 4. The Coaching
"Who is actually running practice, and what is their background?"
*   *Why it matters:* You are paying for instruction, not just a warm body in the dugout.

### 5. The Communication
"How do we handle questions about playing time or player struggles?"
*   *Why it matters:* Clear channels of communication save everyone headaches.

### 6. The Roster
"How many players are on the team, and what is the 'cut' policy?"
*   *Why it matters:* Too many players = less reps. Too few = fatigue.

### 7. The Goals
"What are the long-term goals for this team (showcase vs. local tournament)?"
*   *Why it matters:* Align the team's goals with your athlete's aspirations.

### 8. The Conflict
"What happens if my kid has to miss a tournament for a family event?"
*   *Why it matters:* Life happens. See if they are reasonable.

### 9. The Feedback
"How and when do players get real feedback on their performance?"
*   *Why it matters:* Athletes need to know what to work on.

### 10. The Culture
"Can I talk to a parent from last year’s team?"
*   *Why it matters:* References don't lie.
