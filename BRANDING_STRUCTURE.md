# "The Playbook" Branding Structure

## Tier 1: The Bio Suite (Core Asset)
- The baseline for all coaches/athletes. 
- High-authority, clean template integration.

## Tier 2: The Hall of Fame ("Presence Management")
- **3-Day Pulse (Maintenance):** Steady engagement, keeps the brand visible.
- **5-Day Growth (Acceleration):** Aggressive engagement, focus on follower acquisition.
- **7-Day Authority (Market Takeover):** The "Always-On" machine. High content density, full brand management.

## Included in HOF:
- Bio Suite (Included)
- Monthly Brand Audit
- Content Calendar
