About Coach Card

Charles Card is the owner and head coach of Bomb Squad Baseball, one of Las Vegas' premier competitive baseball programs. With divisions for both youth (14-16U) and adult (18+) players, Bomb Squad develops athletes who compete at the highest levels.

Coach Card's philosophy centers on building complete players — strong fundamentals, mental toughness, and the competitive drive that separates good players from great ones. His teams are known for their discipline, hustle, and never-quit attitude.
