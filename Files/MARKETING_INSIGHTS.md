# Marketing Insights & Selling Points

- **The Hook:** "Wear Your Colors. Build Your Brand." (Team-based theming).
- **The Psychological Advantage:** Instant rapport. When the coach's site colors match the athlete's favorite team, the coach feels like "one of them" before the first pitch is even thrown.
- **The Competitive Edge:** Most coaching sites are boring, static resumes. CoachingBio pages are dynamic, brand-focused experiences.
