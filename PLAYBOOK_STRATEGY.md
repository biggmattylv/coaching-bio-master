# The Playbook: Product Hierarchy

## Tier 1: Rookie (The "Identity" Asset)
- **Deliverable:** Free Professional Bio Card.
- **Value:** Digital identity, authority, "the magnet."
- **Goal:** Lead capture & data gathering.

## Tier 2: All-Star (The "Business" Asset)
- **Deliverable:** Payments + Calendar + Fundraising (Hit-a-thon integration via PhoneSites).
- **Value:** Operational efficiency. They can now transact and raise money.
- **Goal:** Turn the coach/athlete into a functional, revenue-generating business.

## Tier 3: Hall of Fame (The "Marketing" Asset)
- **Deliverable:** Full Presence Management (Socialmonials Enterprise posting).
- **Value:** Market dominance. We take the content load entirely off their shoulders.
- **Goal:** Authority at scale.
